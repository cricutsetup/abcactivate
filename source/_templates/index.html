How to activate ABC TV on Android devices?

  • Firstly, go to the play store or any other store on your Android device.
  • Then search for the ABC TV application in the search bar of the play store.
  • From the search list of play store, select the correct ABC application.
  • Then click on the Install option.
  • Now downloading of application will begin.
  • After downloading the application, install it.
  • And then open the application.
  • Then the sign-in page will open.
  • Now enter your Sign-in details and get into your ABC.com/activate account.
  • If you don't have an account, sign up or create an account on ABC TV.
  • That's it; activation of ABC TV on Android devices is successful.
